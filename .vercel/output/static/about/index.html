About Empereal

A global clean energy and water solutions company delivering engineering-led EPC projects since 2011 — headquartered in Singapore, operating across Asia, the Middle East, and the United States.

Get in Touch View Our Projects

Our Story

Founded to close the gap

Empereal Energy and Services was founded to enable rapidly growing economies to manage a critical infrastructure challenge: the growing gap between demand and supply in energy and fresh water. Headquartered in Singapore, with operations in India, the Middle East, and the United States, Empereal provides design, engineering, procurement, construction, and O&M services for renewable energy and water desalination systems.

Our services are designed to be innovative and reliable, enabling clients to meet their growing requirements in a sustainable and cost-efficient manner.

Our Purpose

Empowering communities through clean energy and pure water

Empereal exists to empower communities through the provision of clean energy and pure water, delivered via innovative and reliable world-class systems. We aim to create deep social impact that improves the quality of life and sustainability of industrial, commercial, and rural communities.

Our expertise allows us to design, build, and operate innovative and reliable renewable energy projects to match any mix of requirements.

  • Trusted EPC expertise across 200+ commissioned projects worldwide
  • Pioneering architectural solar integration across Asia and the Middle East
  • ISO 9001, ISO 14001, and ISO 45001 certified operations
  • Patented solar thermal and solar desalination technologies

Our Engineering Philosophy

Four principles that guide every project

Empereal was modelled to be sustainable without subsidies — a principle established at founding that continues to guide how we select, design, and deliver projects.

Design-first methodology

Every project begins with rigorous feasibility, generation studies, and system modelling before a single piece of equipment is specified or procured.

Quality-certified delivery

ISO 9001:2015, ISO 14001:2015, and ISO 45001:2018 certified management systems govern every aspect of project delivery — design, procurement, construction, and O&M.

Full-lifecycle responsibility

From concept and feasibility through engineering, EPC, commissioning, and long-term O&M — Empereal retains accountability across the full project lifecycle.

Innovation with accountability

Patented solar thermal and desalination technologies backed by 15+ years of operational track record — innovation tested at scale, not in a laboratory.

"Sustainable without subsidies" — a founding principle that continues to guide project selection and business model design at Empereal.

Sustainability Alignment

Contributing to a net-zero future

Every project Empereal delivers contributes directly to decarbonisation targets, water security, or both — measured in megawatts generated, litres of water produced, and tonnes of carbon avoided.

Decarbonisation

Every solar installation reduces fossil fuel dependency — measured, reported, and verified.

Net-zero buildings

BIPV systems that transform building envelopes into energy generators — facades, roofs, railings, and canopies.

Water security

Solar desalination that produces fresh water without carbon emissions — from 20,000 to 150,000+ litres per day.

Green hydrogen

Advancing molecular energy carriers for hard-to-abate sectors via alkaline electrolysis powered by renewables.

ESG contribution

Supporting corporate and government clients in achieving sustainability targets through bankable, certified energy assets.

Global reach

Active operations in Singapore, UAE, India, and the United States — with expansion into Central Asia and new markets.

How We Work

Delivery models

Empereal structures project delivery to match client requirements — from full EPCM mandates to develop-build-own-operate arrangements and standalone technical advisory.

EPCM

Engineering, Procurement, Construction & Management

Full-scope delivery from design through commissioning and handover, with integrated project management.

Project Developer

Develop-Build-Own-Operate

Empereal develops, builds, and operates energy and water assets — providing clients with performance-based offtake structures.

BOO / BOT

Build-Operate-Own or Transfer

Long-term asset ownership or defined transfer arrangements for government and utility-scale clients.

Consulting

Feasibility & Technical Advisory

Feasibility assessments, generation studies, system modelling, and independent technical advisory for lenders and developers.
