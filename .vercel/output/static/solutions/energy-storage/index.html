Bridging intermittency with engineering

Solar energy is intermittent. Supply cannot be.

Solar energy's intermittency requires intelligent storage and management systems to ensure reliable, continuous power. Empereal designs solar PV energy storage with intelligent EMS that manages flow and balances supply-demand. In addition to battery-based storage, Empereal has developed patented thermal storage solutions using phase-change materials for continuous solar heat availability — for power generation and desalination, even when sunlight is not.

Two storage technologies

Electrical and thermal — matched to the application

Empereal designs storage systems as an integral part of the overall energy system — not as an afterthought. Technology selection is driven by the specific application, load profile, and whether the storage function is electrical or thermal.

  • Electrical Storage

    Battery Energy Storage Systems (BESS)

    Battery storage integrated directly with solar PV — enabling peak shaving, load shifting, off-grid operation, and grid stability support. Paired with intelligent energy management that monitors generation, storage state, and demand in real time.

    Electrical power storage & dispatch
    Configuration

    Management

    Integration

  • Thermal Storage — Patented

    Phase-Change Material (PCM) Thermal Storage

    Patented thermal energy storage using phase-change materials that absorb, store, and release heat at precisely controlled temperatures. Enables continuous operation of solar thermal — including desalination and power generation — independent of solar intermittency.

Patented PCM Thermal Storage

Continuous solar heat — day and night.

Empereal's phase-change material thermal storage system is a patented innovation that addresses the fundamental limitation of solar thermal systems: intermittency. By storing heat in materials that change phase at precisely defined temperatures, the system releases a consistent, controlled heat output over extended periods — even after sunset or during cloud cover.

This technology is integral to Empereal's solar desalination systems, enabling round-the-clock fresh water production without battery storage or fossil fuel backup — at a fraction of the capital cost of equivalent electrical storage.

  • Stores heat at precisely controlled phase-change temperatures
  • 4+ hour thermal storage duration — no battery required
  • Fraction of the cost of equivalent electrical storage
  • Enables 24/7 desalination and process heat — zero fossil fuel
  • Proprietary Empereal technology — patented and operational

Engineering principles

Three principles guiding every storage system

Storage is not a product — it is a system design decision. Empereal's approach ensures every storage solution is correctly specified, integrated, and managed for its specific application.

  • 01

    System integration first

    Storage is designed as part of the overall energy system, not as an add-on component. Thermal and electrical storage are sized and optimised for the specific application — load profile, solar resource, and dispatch requirements.

  • 02

    Patented thermal storage

    Our phase-change material storage systems provide reliable thermal energy storage at a fraction of the cost of battery storage for equivalent thermal applications — proprietary technology proven in operational plants.

  • 03

    Intelligent management

    Real-time energy management systems monitor generation, storage levels, and demand to optimise dispatch and ensure uninterrupted supply — whether grid-connected, off-grid, or hybrid.

Applications

Six storage application sectors

Empereal's energy storage solutions are deployed across six primary application contexts — from grid-connected commercial facilities to fully off-grid resorts and industrial sites.

  • Grid-connected solar + storage

    Peak shaving, load shifting, and grid stability support for commercial and industrial facilities — reducing demand charges and optimising self-consumption.

  • Off-grid & remote locations

    24/7 power for sites beyond grid reach — solar PV + BESS + controlled diesel backup, sized to eliminate or dramatically reduce diesel consumption.

  • CSP & thermal power plants

    Molten salt and accumulator storage for concentrating solar power — enabling dispatchable renewable electricity generation beyond daylight hours.

  • Desalination systems

    PCM thermal storage enabling round-the-clock solar desalination — continuous fresh water without fossil fuel backup or large battery arrays.

  • Industrial facilities

    Reliable solar power with storage for manufacturing — ensuring production continuity and eliminating exposure to grid outages.

  • Resorts & islands

    Fully renewable energy systems with storage for off-grid hospitality — eliminating diesel dependency and providing genuinely sustainable guest experiences.

How we deliver

Our storage system delivery process

Storage system design begins with the load — not the technology. Every Empereal storage project is scoped to match the specific energy demand, solar resource, and operational requirements of the site.

  1. 1

    Load & resource analysis

    Demand profiling, solar resource assessment, and storage duration requirement modelling.

  2. 2

    Technology selection & sizing

    BESS vs thermal storage selection, capacity sizing, EMS specification, and system architecture design.

  3. 3

    Integration & commissioning

    Storage integration with solar generation, grid or off-grid connection, controls programming, and performance testing.

  4. 4

    Monitoring & optimisation

    Real-time EMS monitoring, storage performance reporting, degradation management, and system optimisation.

This proprietary technology is deployed in commissioned plants and is integral to Empereal's solar desalination systems.
