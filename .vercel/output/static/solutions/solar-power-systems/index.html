Full-lifecycle solar EPC

From rooftop to utility-scale, every system engineered to perform.

Empereal designs, engineers, procures, constructs, and commissions solar PV systems across the full project scale — from distributed rooftop installations to multi-megawatt utility-scale solar farms. With over 650 MWp of solar PV capacity installed and 200+ projects delivered, we provide bankable solar generation that meets grid codes, regulatory requirements, and long-term performance targets.

Scope of services

What we deliver

Empereal's solar EPC scope spans the full project lifecycle — from early-stage feasibility through to long-term asset operations and performance optimisation.

  • Utility-scale solar PV farms

    Development and EPC for large-scale ground-mounted PV farms, including tracker systems and grid connection.

  • Commercial & industrial rooftops

    High-yield rooftop solar for factories, warehouses, offices, and commercial buildings — optimised for self-consumption and export.

  • Solar carports & parking structures

    Integrated solar carport and parking canopy installations that combine shading infrastructure with energy generation.

  • Ground-mounted & fixed-tilt systems

    Fixed-tilt PV systems engineered for maximum yield, land efficiency, and long-term reliability.

  • Single-axis tracker installations

    Tracker-based systems for utility-scale applications where additional generation yield justifies the investment.

  • Feasibility & generation modelling

    Bankable pre-development studies — resource assessment, P50/P90 generation modelling, and shadow analysis.

  • Electrical balance-of-plant design

    BOP electrical design based on local grid codes including SHAMS/D33 for Dubai BIPV systems.

  • Authority approvals & interconnection

    Full regulatory submission management, utility interconnection, and commissioning authority approvals.

  • Architectural BIPV

    Custom-designed solar facades, railings, canopies, and building-integrated modules for landmark architecture.

  • Operations & maintenance services

    Long-term O&M: performance monitoring, preventive maintenance, fault response, and yield reporting.

Architectural BIPV

Solar as architecture, not an afterthought.

Empereal is one of a small number of global firms with the engineering capability to design, certify, and install building-integrated photovoltaic systems that are simultaneously high-performance generators and integral architectural elements.

Our BIPV work spans facades, roofs, railings, canopies, and bespoke solar elements engineered to meet IEC module certification standards and building-code requirements simultaneously.

  • IEC 61730 & IEC 61215 solar module certification
  • Building material certifications: C1048, E2353, E935, E1886
  • Dubai SHAMS/D33 compliant system design
  • Net-zero building sustainability rating support
  • 40+ BIPV projects at Expo 2020 Dubai alone

  • Canopies
  • Custom modules

Applications

Six solar application types

Empereal's solar EPC capability spans the full range — from distributed residential systems to utility-scale assets and iconic architectural integrations.

  • Utility-scale generation

    Multi-MW solar farms feeding into national grids. Fixed-tilt and tracker configurations. From 1 MW to 110 MWp+ delivered.

  • Commercial & industrial rooftops

    Energy cost reduction for factories, warehouses, and offices — optimised for self-consumption with surplus export to the grid.

  • Government & public buildings

    Net-zero public infrastructure with architectural solar integration — meeting green building standards and public sector targets.

  • Transportation infrastructure

    Solar-powered bus depots, metro facilities, tram depots, and airport energy systems — including 11 MW across 12 RTA sites in Dubai.

  • Residential & mixed-use

    Solar carports, balcony solar railings, and building-integrated facades for residential towers and master-planned communities.

  • Iconic architecture

    Custom-designed solar modules integrated into landmark buildings — from the UAE Pavilion at Expo 2020 to the world's largest net-zero resort.

How we deliver

Our solar EPC process

Every solar project follows a rigorous phased process managed under ISO 9001:2015, ISO 14001:2015, and ISO 45001:2018 certified management systems.

  1. 1

    Feasibility & design

    Site assessment, resource modelling, generation studies, shading analysis, and concept system design.

  2. 2

    Engineering & procurement

    Detailed electrical and structural engineering, equipment specification, tendering, and supply chain management.

  3. 3

    Construction & commissioning

    Field installation, testing, grid connection, authority approvals, and handover documentation.

  4. 4

    Operations & maintenance

    Long-term O&M — performance monitoring, preventive maintenance, fault response, and yield reporting.
